Founded in 2008, Cyber Craft Solutions is now 600 people with over 35 international clients, primarily in Western Europe and North America. A Boston office was recently opened to better accommodate American clients.

Cyber Craft Solutions embraced Agile Transformation three years ago. Our LACE (Lean Agile Center for Excellence) coalition works with companies to educate, implement, and support their teams throughout their agile journeys. We have trained over 1000 people including our own colleagues, clients, and those in communities in which we serve. We are agile-driven throughout all development teams and support services.

Philosophy
Our philosophy is rooted in respect and empathy which guides how we manage all our relationships. This is at the heart of our progress.
Clients
We foster clients with mutual affinity which leads to more solid, longer-term partnerships. As such, the lines between client and service provider are blurred, and we operate as a unified whole.
Colleagues
Cyber Craft Solutions has learned that by investing value and faith in our people, they, in turn, invest that value in solving clients’ issues. This means they take ownership for projects and determine means of fulfilling client needs for the best end result.
Gender Parity
Cyber Craft Solutions has a reputation for aiming at a 50/50 gender split in response to a more natural and harmonious work… environment. Colleagues are accustomed to lack of gender bias, and women have always had equivalent in salaries and career possibilities.

Culture

While many of our esteemed local competitors are capable service providers, most would agree that differentiating factors usually center around culture. This means that what we do can be of equal quality, but the culture in which people live and work every day determines their preference of one environment over another.

This applies equally to clients and colleagues.

Because clients feel the warm, open, welcoming spirit of Cyber Craft Solutions and the creative and free environment, they feel at home with their Cyber Craft teams and company leadership.

People and Culture

Cyber Craft Solutions’ distinct culture is the open, welcoming spirit, the freedom to be oneself, and the creative pride from the intrinsic link between technology and music. Adding to this spirit of freedom is the workplace home Cyber Craft Solutions creates in the Dutch-inspired office, designed around an open park setting and the Amsterdam Bar, where people love to congregate for coffee, lunch, or beer on casual Fridays.
